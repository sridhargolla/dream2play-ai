# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making Dream2Play AI a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive constructive feedback with care.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, documentation, and outcomes.
- Prioritize the safety and privacy of users and contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make the project unsafe or hostile.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project repositories, issue trackers, merge requests, discussions, community spaces, and any project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close threads, reject contributions, restrict repository access, or take other proportionate action when this Code of Conduct is violated.

Reports should be made privately to the project maintainers through the repository owner or project group. Reports will be reviewed with discretion and respect for all parties involved.

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards, applying this Code of Conduct fairly, and protecting the integrity of the project community.
